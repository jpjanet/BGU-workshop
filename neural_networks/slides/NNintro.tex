Neural networks are a family of nonlinear models distinguished by:
\vspace{0.5cm}
\begin{enumerate}
	\item \visible<2->{High, 'controllable', degree of nonlinearity (c.f. universal approximation theorems).\vspace{0.5cm}}
	\item \visible<3->{Continuity, differentiability (high Lipschitz constant)\vspace{0.5cm}}
	\item \visible<4->{Easy access to cheap derivatives!\vspace{0.5cm}}
	\item \visible<5->{Cheap at evaluation time\vspace{0.5cm}}
	\item \visible<6->{easily scaled for different input structures.\vspace{0.5cm}}
\end{enumerate}
