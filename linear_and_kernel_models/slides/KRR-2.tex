The most widely used kernel is the Gaussian kernel:
\begin{align*}
K_{i,j} = \exp\left(-\sigma^{-2}\left\Vert x_i-x_j \right\Vert_2^2 \right)
\end{align*}
\only<2->{What is $\varphi(x_i)$ here?}
\only<3->{\begin{align*}
\varphi(x) = e^{\frac{-x^2}{2\sigma^2}}\begin{bmatrix} 1 &
&{\frac{1}{\sigma}}x &\sqrt{\frac{1}{2!\sigma^4}}x^2 &\hdots\end{bmatrix}^T
\end{align*}
Actually a Hilbert space (infinite dimensions...)}
